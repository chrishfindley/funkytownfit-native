## Description (App Store — 4,000 chars max)

Funkytown Fit is the fitness app that actually knows you.

No generic plans. No confusing interfaces. Just a smart, personalized training and nutrition system that adapts to your goals, your schedule, and your life.

**YOUR WORKOUT PROGRAM, BUILT FOR YOU**
Tell Funkytown Fit your goals — lose fat, build muscle, run a 5K, boost strength — and it generates a complete multi-week training plan tailored to your experience level, available equipment, and how many days a week you can commit. Every exercise comes with clear instructions and the science behind why it's in your program.

**TRACK EVERY REP, EVERY SET**
Log workouts in real time with a clean, fast interface built for the gym floor. Rest timers, RPE tracking, automatic 1-rep max estimates, and a complete history of every session you've ever done.

**NUTRITION, SIMPLIFIED**
Your daily calorie and macro targets are calculated from your profile. Log food by searching our database, scanning a barcode, or using our AI food photo feature — just point your camera at your meal and let Claude AI identify it and fill in the nutrition automatically.

**PROGRESS YOU CAN SEE**
Watch your weight trend downward with Garmin-style charts. Track weekly training load with productive/maintenance/overreaching zones. See your all-time streak, personal bests, and total volume moved.

**YOUR DATA, YOUR CLOUD**
Create an account and your data syncs securely across devices. Switch phones, share with a training partner, or pick up right where you left off — everything is always backed up.

**BUILT FOR REAL PEOPLE**
- Works for lifters, runners, cyclists, and hybrid athletes
- Supports home gym, dumbbells-only, full gym, and bodyweight training
- Dark, distraction-free UI designed for focus
- No subscriptions. No ads. Just fitness.
